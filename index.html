    About

    I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the Johns Hopkins University, where I am fortunate to be mentored by Professor Carey Priebe at the Department of Applied Mathematics and Statistics and Professor Nilanjan Chatterjee at the Department of Biostatistics. Previously, I was a graduate student at the Department of Statistics at the University of Wisconsin–Madison, where I was honored to be supervised by Professor Joshua Cape and Professor Vivak Patel. My institutional website can be accessed at cis.jhu.edu, stat.wisc.edu and cs.wisc.edu.
